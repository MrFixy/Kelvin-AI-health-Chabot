What we believe

Our values

Compassion first

Every design decision starts with one question: does this feel kind? We build for the person at their most vulnerable, not their most confident.

Privacy always

Your conversations belong to you. We will never sell, share, or monetise what you tell Kelvin. End-to-end encryption is non-negotiable.

Always available

Mental health doesn't keep office hours. Kelvin is there at 2am, on weekends, on public holidays — whenever you need someone to talk to.

Human-centred AI

We believe AI is a powerful complement to human care, not a replacement for it. Kelvin always encourages professional support when it's needed most.
